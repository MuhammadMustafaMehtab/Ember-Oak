# Ember & Oak — Fine Dining Restaurant Landing Page

A modern, fully responsive restaurant landing page designed to showcase a fine dining brand's menu, ambiance, and story. Built with a warm, elegant dark theme accented in gold, the page is structured to convert visitors into reservations through clear calls-to-action and an immersive visual experience.

---

## 📖 About This Project

Ember & Oak is a single-page restaurant website concept that highlights:

- A bold hero section with a reservation-focused call-to-action
- Key business stats (years of experience, dishes, guests served, ratings)
- A curated signature menu with pricing and quick "Add to Cart" actions
- A brand story section narrating the restaurant's culinary journey
- A photo gallery capturing the food, ambiance, and interiors
- Customer testimonials to build trust and social proof
- A dedicated reservation call-to-action banner
- A detailed footer with quick links, opening hours, and contact information

The layout is fully responsive, ensuring a consistent and polished experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
